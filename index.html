Who I am

Software Developer | Passion for Mentoring | House Plant Enthusiast

My personal philosophy in life is that everything is a learning experience. Some of my favorite things to learn have been painting, gardening and programming! I am not afraid to make mistakes, as they are often the most powerful tools in learning. My enthusiasm for learning stems from my passion for mentoring. This passion has translated over into my software development, and has even lead to winning an award. In my first ever hackathon, I worked on a team where I acted in Project Management and Software Developer roles to develop the website breakingthrough.tech. The website's purpose was to provide recources for marginalized women in tech, and match them with industry mentors. This website was awarded Cisco's "Best #InclusiveFuture Hack". After seeing my passion project go live, I knew without a doubt that making my switch to tech was the right path for me.

In my previous career as a dental hygienist, I developed many skills that help me today as a software developer. Some of these skills include time management, effective communication, group collaboration, and attention to detail. I have a passion for writing clean, testable code and have had so much fun building side projects in Java. I am looking for a software developer role where I can bring my passions and skills to a team where we can create impactful projects together. I highly value companies that place an emphasis on diversity and inclusion in the work place, and I can't wait to get out there and start creating thoughtful solutions to complex problems!
